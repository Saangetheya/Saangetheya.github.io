About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Jose-Luis Ambite. I work as a Researcher in the AI Division at the Information Sciences Institute, where I specialize in 3D imaging, generative AI, and multimodal learning. My work spans several biomedical domains, including neuroimaging — 3D brain MRI generation and imputation, and ophthalmology, where I develop glaucoma triaging algorithms. Prior to USC, I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Technology Tirupati.
